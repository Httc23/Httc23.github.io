    Corporate Spirit

    Focus on safety and stick to peace.

    Our developed systems are designed to assist industries in achieving "safety" management. This stems from Hon-Tsai Technology's focus on three technical areas: "mobile devices," "safety management," and "IoT," We embrace "Research and Development for Innovation Internationalization" and "Professional High-quality Safety" as our company's core values and distinctive features. We follow the principles of Success, Attitude, Focus, and Execute to assist businesses in implementing successful systems.
