    How To Help Businesses

    Make safety “real-time, visible, and traceable”

    Hon-Tsai Real-Time Hazardous Construction Video System sets a new standard for intelligent construction supervision. Once a video is uploaded, it is automatically tagged for easy searching, tracing, and analysis. With support for both web and app platforms, it provides instant push notifications to owners, ensuring no delay in monitoring on-site conditions. Utilizing modular deployment and mobile recording devices, installation requires no wiring and offers flexible viewing angles, allowing for rapid deployment across different sites. This not only reduces setup costs but also makes hazardous construction supervision more agile and efficient.

    Problem

    Information is scattered

    Video files and inspection forms are separate and difficult to integrate.

    Difficulty in Searching

    Reviewing historical assignments requires searching through multiple systems and folders.

    After-the-fact remedy

    No real-time video, unable to monitor the scene immediately.

    Compliance Challenges

    Difficulty in providing complete, contextual evidence during audits.

    Design Concept

    • Real-Time Supervision：Instant transmission of construction footage, ensuring transparency in high-risk construction activities.
    • Smart Marking：Automatically add tags for more efficient video retrieval and report analysis.
    • Mobility：Portable recording device with adjustable angles to adapt to complex construction environments.
    • Industrial Protection：Explosion-proof, dust-proof, and waterproof; supports humid environments and strong outdoor light.
    • Module Deployment：Non-permanent design, reusable, reducing construction costs.
    • Central Control Center：Centralized management allows owners to simultaneously monitor the status of multiple construction sites.

    Feature

    Industrial standard mobile equipment

    • Multi-site reuse: The device can be repeatedly carried to different construction sites, reducing material waste and costs.
    • Ready to use: No wiring installation required, enabling quick deployment and shortening construction preparation time.
    • Flexible scheduling: The camera angle can be adjusted in real-time, providing comprehensive recording of the construction process.
    • Industrial-grade design: Features industrial protection with IP68 dust and water resistance, supports glove and wet touch operation, suitable for strong light or outdoor use, and various hazardous construction environments.

    Real-time monitoring system

    • Real-time Streaming Video: Construction footage is uploaded with zero delay, capturing every detail without missing anything.
    • Cross-Platform Synchronization: Fully supported on smartphones, tablets, and computers, allowing owners to remotely monitor at any time.
    • Centralized Management of Multiple Construction Sites: One platform can oversee multiple construction locations simultaneously, making unified management more efficient.
    • Playback Function: Access historical video records anytime for easy tracking and review.

    Systematic File Storage

    • Intelligent Archiving: Automatic classification and access of images and documents for fast and thorough retrieval.
    • Centralized Data Management: Systematic integration of dispersed image data to reduce management workload.
    • Process Tracking: Complete preservation of construction phase records, enabling quick retrieval of key image data to ensure a well-documented construction process.

    Ongoing Support Services

    • Technical Support: Providing technical consultation and troubleshooting.
    • Regular System Maintenance: Routine system health checks, performance optimization, and security updates.
    • Training Services: Staff training, advanced feature instruction, and best practice sharing.
    • Version Upgrade Services: Continuous feature updates and regulatory compliance maintenance.
