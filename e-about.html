    Let us show you our capabilities.

    Hon-tsai Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 2010 and is dedicated to creating customized security software for businesses. Our service is available for government(Labor Inspection Office), petrochemical industry(CPC Corporation,Taiwan, CHIMEI Corporation), construction industry (CTCI Co., Ltd.), electronics industry(GlobalWafers Co., Ltd., AUO Corporation), transportation industry(Taoyuan International Airport Co. Ltd.), hospital (Taipei Veterans General Hospital, Taichung Veterans General Hospital,Kaohsiung Veterans General Hospital), etc. We established the system of inspection and auditing for every place, things, and equipment and automatically send the notification of abnormal messages to rise the control and management of the manager in the construction site through the mobile devices and information and communication technology. We offer the trend data analysis of each equipment to reach the management of best pre-control and correction.
