    How To Help Businesses

    Establish a digital inspection mechanism where inspection personnel leave records wherever they go.

    Mobile duty supervision is a "value-added" and "quality-enhancing" activity. It ensures that work is carried out effectively and inspections are conducted thoroughly and provides the client with a high-quality, convenient way to check the current status. Our company has integrated mobile security patrol operations with duty inspection to develop a new "Mobile Duty Supervision Management System generation." This system utilizes smart handheld or wearable devices to transmit results and report any anomalies to relevant personnel instantly. The system replaces traditional paper-based inspection operations during epidemic periods, reducing human contact and providing faster on-site situation awareness.

    Problems

    Is duty inspection still stuck in the recording stage on paper, scanning for digital storage?

    Or relying on memory to type on a computer?

    Transformation

    Form Items

    The form items are built according to legal regulations to facilitate automatic inspections and generate form information.

    Paperless, Mobile Inspection

    We have designed a mobile device-based inspection plan to enable inspection personnel to carry out their tasks in a portable manner (as shown in the diagram, with screens for both smartphones and smartwatches).

    Report Abnormalities on the Spot

    During duty inspections, if you encounter any abnormal situations, you can use the camera to capture the abnormalities and report them for a specific inspection item, clearly indicating the location of the problem. Supervisors can simultaneously access abnormal reports on their mobile devices.

    Supervisory Audits with Easy-to-view Records

    Supervisors can use their mobile devices to check the last inspection records of equipment and whether any deficiency improvement forms have been issued, allowing them to review the records of equipment.

    Automated Report Generation Based on Scoring

    Supervisors can conduct audits and scoring using their mobile devices after setting the required audit items and scoring criteria in the system back end. Once the data is submitted, the system automatically calculates the scores and generates scoring reports.

    Feature

    • Support for Multiple Devices: Assist businesses in implementing inspection operations by supporting various front end patrolling devices, such as smartphones and smartwatches.
    • Significantly reduce the error rate: Significant Reduction in the Impact of Inexperienced Patrol Personnel.
    • Real-time Abnormality Reporting: Rapidly respond to abnormalities in the patrolled areas for immediate action by management.
