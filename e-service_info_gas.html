    How To Help Businesses

    Real-time data, intelligent monitoring, workplace safety protection

    Traditional gas detection operations can only be conducted on-site and in real-time, meaning managers often learn about the situation only afterward or when an incident occurs. The Hongcai mobile gas detection system can automatically and continuously transmit data, allowing remote monitoring at a glance. The system automatically interprets the data and provides real-time alerts, enabling managers to immediately grasp any abnormalities. This not only reduces risks but also offers on-site personnel real-time safety protection.

    | Smart protection, real-time defense |

    Problem

    Unable to check the on-site situation in real time

    Gas testing is only conducted at the site and cannot transmit data back, so the client cannot confirm whether it has been properly carried out.

    Risk of Misjudgment

    Detectors require regular calibration; insufficient maintenance can easily lead to measurement errors, misjudgments, or delayed responses.

    Intermittent Testing

    Most processes involve testing before entry or at scheduled intervals, making continuous monitoring impossible.

    Insufficient information recording

    Most records are kept manually, lacking complete digital files, making follow-up difficult.

    Design Concept

    Real-time monitoring, automatic information reporting

    • Continuous uploading of gas measurement data for full-process monitoring of the gas environment.
    • Abnormal alert notifications to ensure management personnel receive warning information immediately.
    • Emergency evacuation reminders to ensure the safety of workers.

    Industrial-grade equipment with fully enhanced performance

    • Long-lasting rechargeable battery, supporting extended continuous monitoring.
    • IP68 protection rating, waterproof and dustproof, suitable for various hazardous environments.
    • Industrial-grade, supports glove and wet touch operation, ideal for harsh workplace safety condition.

    Complete Preservation of Historical Information with Automatic Generation of Analytical Charts

    • The system automatically saves data, including a timeline record, facilitating audits and incident investigations.
    • Automatically generates line charts and statistical graphs to provide real-time insights into environmental trends.
    • Reduces manual record-keeping and paper management, achieving full digitalization of data.

    Feature

    • Industrial-grade mobile equipment, high-performance monitoring：High-performance battery for long-lasting real-time monitoring and alert triggering.
    • Real-time Safety Management：Test data is uploaded in real-time to assist with rapid response and disaster prevention.
    • Remote supervision to ensure a safe working environment：The system fully records the gas measurement execution status, allowing managers to remotely monitor and ensure that on-site gas measurement tasks are properly carried out.
