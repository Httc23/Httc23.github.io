    1.Scope of Privacy Policy

    The contents of the privacy policy include how this website handles personal identification data collected when you use the website's services. This privacy policy does not apply to related websites outside of this site, and it also does not apply to personnel who are not commissioned or involved in the management of this site.

    2.Collection, Processing, and Utilization of Personal Data

    When you visit this website or use the functionality services provided by this website, we will ask you to provide necessary personal data based on the nature of the service. We will process and utilize your personal data within the scope of the specific purpose. Without your written consent, this website will not use personal data for other purposes.

    When you use interactive features such as service mailboxes and questionnaires, this website will retain the information you provide, including your name, email address, contact information, and usage time.

    During general browsing, the server will automatically record relevant behaviors, including your IP address, usage time, the browser you use, and records of your browsing and clicking data. This information is used for internal purposes and will not be disclosed to the public.

    To provide precise services, we will conduct statistics and analysis on the collected questionnaire survey content. The statistical data or explanatory text resulting from the analysis will be presented. Besides internal research, we may publish statistical data and explanatory text as needed, but it will not involve specific personal data.

    3.Data Protection

    The host servers of this website are equipped with information security equipment, including firewalls and antivirus systems, as well as other relevant information security devices and necessary security protection measures. We employ strict protective measures to safeguard both the website and your personal data. Only authorized personnel are allowed to access your personal data, and these personnel have signed confidentiality agreements. Violations of the confidentiality obligations will result in appropriate legal consequences.

    If it is necessary to entrust other units to provide services due to business needs, this website will also strictly require them to comply with confidentiality obligations. Necessary inspection procedures will be implemented to ensure that they will indeed comply with these obligations.

    4.Related Links Outside the Website

    The web pages of this website provide internet links to other websites. You can also enter other websites through the links provided by this website. However, the privacy protection policy of this website does not apply to these websites. You must refer to the privacy protection policy in the linked website.

    5.Policy on Sharing Personal Data with Third Parties

    This website will never provide, exchange, rent, or sell any of your personal data to other individuals, groups, private enterprises, or government agencies. However, exceptions may occur based on legal obligations or contractual obligations.

    6.Use of Cookies

    To provide you with the best service, this website will place and access cookies on your computer. If you do not want to accept cookies, you can set the privacy level to high in your browser's function options. This will refuse the writing of cookies but may result in certain functions of the website not functioning properly.

    7.Revision of Privacy Policy

    This website's privacy protection policy will be revised as needed, and the amended terms will be published on the website.
